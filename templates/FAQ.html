What are the controls?

To move the player use the WASD keys.

To cast offensive spells use 1 to 6.

To cast defensive spells use G and H.

Once a spell is selected aim and click to cast it.

Is it possible to change the keybinds?

It is currently not possible to change the keybinds but prehaps in the future this feature will be added.

My FPS is extremely low how I fix this?

Currently the game is not optimised for low end devices and there is no guidelines for improving your FPS other than to wait for the optimised version.

Where is multiplayer mode?

Multiplayer mode will be added at a later date. For the time being feel free to test everything out in tutorial mode.

Why is the tutorial so easy?

The AI in the tutorial is not designed to be particularly difficult in order to allow the player to test out the various game concepts at a slow pace. It is possible to increase the difficultly by using the chat commands.

What do the chat commands do?

The chat commands (!Left, !Right, !Top, !Bot) changes the AI movement pattern to fly across the grid rather than at the player. For a challenge try using 3 out of 4 commands and then running in the opposite direction of the remaining command direction i.e if !Right is the only command remaining try running left.

What do the spells actually do?

Fireball: is a classic mage spell that simply does damage upon collision.


Frostbolt: inflicts damage and freezes the enemy in place for 6 seconds.


Splitter: splits into 8 projectiles that all inflict damage.


Recharger: inflicts damage and it's cooldown is reset on hit.


Molten Boulder: inflicts damage to all enemies caught in its path.


Warlock's Sigil: spawns a zone that temporarily slows down enemies.


Deflection Shield: surrounds the caster in a bubble that blocks all damage.


Cauterize: Heals the caster for a certain amount.

How much damage does each spell inflict?

Fireball: 5 Damage.

Frostbolt: 5 Damage.

Splitter: 5 Damage.

Splitter Projectiles: 3 Damage each.

Recharger: 5 Damage.

Molten Boulder: 10 Damage.

Warlock's Sigil: 0 Damage - utility spell.

Cauterize: Heals for either 10 or 20 health.


These values are not set in stone and are subject to change for multiplayer mode.
